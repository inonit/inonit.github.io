Are you In On IT?

Inonit AS is a IT-consultancy company based in Oslo, Norway. We do regular consultancy-work as well as develop our own projects. Our goal is to primarily focus on open source solutions, and be a social hub in Norway for the Free-software movement.

Inonit AS is a strong believer in Python and particularly Django, and strive to deliver solutions in this framework.
